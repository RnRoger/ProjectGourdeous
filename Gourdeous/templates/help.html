Help page


On this page we will give an introduction to this application.
In the following steps we will give you an example on how to use the application.

Home page
The background information of the project is explained on this page.

Sunburst
This page contains the visualisation. A sunburst is shown with multiple functions. When you double click on the sunburst, it will
zoom in. Click on the slice you want to know more of. With the highlight function corresponding words are shown. This makes it easier
to see what organisms share the same health effects or compounds. With the searchbox you can find words fast in the sunburst. This way
you don't have to look through the whole sunburst. When you click once on a slice, articles will be shown on the right size of the burst
in a table. In the table there are titles of articles that contains the health effect or compound where you've clicked on. The titles are
links to PubMed.

Textminer
On this page you can execute textmining. Type a plant in the first searchbox. In the second searchbox type a health effect and give your
e-mail address. This will be used to notify the user if something goes wrong. Click on the "GoMine" button to start textmining. The keywords
will be used to go through articles on PubMed. When the keywords are found in the articles, these keywords and articles will be added to the
database. The output will be shown in the sunburst.
